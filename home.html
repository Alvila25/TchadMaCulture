{% trans "Cuisine and Taste of Tradition" %}

{% trans "Traditional Dishes & Recipes" %}

{% blocktrans %} Chadian cuisine is a delightful blend of African, Arab, and French influences, offering a variety of hearty and flavorful dishes. One of the most iconic meals is Bouillie, a traditional porridge made from millet, often enjoyed as breakfast. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Another staple is Daraba, a nutritious vegetable stew cooked with okra, tomatoes, and peanuts. Kisra, a thin sorghum-based flatbread, is commonly served with rich sauces. Grilled meats, such as Chadian-style lamb skewers, are also popular across the country. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Food & Cultural Identity" %}
